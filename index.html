How to get the most out of Voxai
The difference between a generic post and one that gets 50 comments is the data you give the AI, not the model.
Why does content come out generic?

The AI knows nothing about you. The green field is the key — tell it exactly what happened with real numbers and outcomes.

Recommended flow
  1. Create your profile — Profiles → define who you are, your voice and audience.
  2. Upload documents — Inside the profile → Documents tab → add brand guidelines, decks.
  3. Add style samples — Profile → Style samples → paste 3-5 posts → Analyze style.
  4. Use Topic ideas — Profile → Topic ideas → search trends or AI suggestions.
  5. Use Prompt builder — Profile → Prompt builder → get an optimized prompt → use it.
  6. Select active profile — Top toolbar → Profile dropdown.
  7. Fill in real facts — The green field in each form is the most important thing.
